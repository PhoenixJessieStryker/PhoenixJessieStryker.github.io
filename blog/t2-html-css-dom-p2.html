What is grid based design?

When we look at a website.. our eyes follow a pattern. You might already know what i mean. ..If you dont well.. Let me Explain. you see our eyes when we first look at a page quite often start from the top of the left hand side of the page. We then following this patern look left to right ,then up and down.
Alot of print media follow this example and use it to their advantage, which answers questions as to why the website title is always on the top of the page rather than on the sides.
we see this in alot of websites but to break it down even further... Title - topImage - below the title to the left Text - to the right of the image and so fourth with possible credits to the web developer(s)
